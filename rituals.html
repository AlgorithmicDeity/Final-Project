Rituals & Offerings

Honor the gods, spirits, and the cycles of the year with personalized rituals and offerings.

Wheel of the Year Rituals

Yule

Celebrate the Winter Solstice with light, feasting, and honoring rebirth.

Dísablót

Honor the Disir and fertility as winter ends, welcoming spring's return.

Midsummer

Celebrate the sun's peak power, honoring abundance and light.

Freyfaxi

Give thanks for the first harvest and honor Freyr, god of fertility.

Winternights

Welcome winter by honoring the Disir, ancestors, and land spirits.

Alfablot

Honor the Alfar (spirits of the ancestors and land) in quiet reverence.

Norse Gods & Goddesses

Odin

God of wisdom, war, and poetry.

Thor

God of thunder, strength, and protection.

Freyja

Goddess of love, magic, and beauty.

Frigg

Goddess of motherhood, foresight, and home.

Loki

God of chaos, mischief, and transformation.

Balder

God of light, purity, and rebirth.

Tyr

God of justice, honor, and courage.

Heimdall

Guardian of the Bifrost and the gods.

Njord

God of the sea, wind, and wealth.

Frey

God of fertility, prosperity, and peace.

Skadi

Goddess of winter, hunting, and mountains.

Hel

Goddess of the underworld and the dead.

Bragi

God of poetry, music, and storytelling.

Idun

Goddess of youth and keeper of the apples of immortality.

Ullr

God of winter, skiing, and archery.

Sif

Goddess of earth, fertility, and harvest.

Ran

Goddess of the sea who collects drowned sailors.

Aegir

God of the ocean and sea storms.

Nerthus

Earth goddess of fertility and peace.

Hodr

Blind god of darkness and winter.

Vidar

God of vengeance and silence.

Valkyries

Choosers of the slain who guide warriors to Valhalla.

Norns

Weavers of fate and destiny.
